I was born in Kerala, India with the name Cherry. Cherry was a pretty common name in Kerala & I was actually named after my grandfather (his name was Cherian).

Unfortunately when my family moved to the US, my parents quickly realized that they had named their son after a fruit... So in an attempt to spare me a life of ridicule, they renamed me to Ryan. ('Ryan' comes from the 'rian' in 'Cherian')

Unfortunately, despite their efforts, I found plenty of other ways to ridicule myself over the years (I had a particularly embarrasing hairdo throughout highschool/college). So ultimately I resolved to just stick with Cherry.

The end result? I'm officially Ryan on paper, but everyone just calls me Cherry!

..which is good, I prefer Cherry :)
